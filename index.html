About Us

The Seed of Life Group of Companies Ltd. An African Dream and Possibility Initiative Founded in 2017, The Seed of Life Group of Companies is a trusted supplier and one-stop solution for a range of essential products and services. With a passion for creativity and a commitment to client satisfaction we have grown to become an industry leader.

Our brand is built on continuous investment in quality and innovation, allowing us to provide customized solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. Backed by over five years of experience, we confidently offer high-quality service and competitive pricing to all our valued customers.

Our Services

Stationery

We are your trusted partner for premium stationery solutions, offering a wide range of products to meet your personal, educational, and business needs. From essential office supplies to creative tools, we deliver quality and reliability to help you stay organized and inspired.

Office Consumables

We are your go-to supplier for premium office consumables, offering a wide range of products designed to keep your workspace efficient and productive. From everyday essentials to specialized items, we ensure quality and affordability to meet the demands of modern businesses.

Personal Protective Equipment

We are a trusted supplier of high-quality Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), dedicated to ensuring safety and compliance in every workplace. From head-to-toe protection to industry-specific gear, we provide reliable solutions that prioritize your health and well-being.

Carports

We specialize in providing durable and stylish carports designed to protect your vehicles from harsh weather conditions. With a focus on quality materials and expert craftsmanship, our carports offer reliable protection while enhancing the aesthetic of your property.

Event Equipment Hire

We provide top-quality event equipment hire services to make your occasions unforgettable. From elegant furniture and decor to sound systems and lighting, our diverse inventory ensures you have everything you need to create a seamless and memorable event experience.

Corporate Branding

We offer comprehensive corporate branding solutions designed to elevate your business identity. From custom promotional products to professional signage and branded materials, we help your company stand out, leaving a lasting impression on clients and stakeholders.

Mobile Toilets Hire

We provide reliable and hygienic mobile toilet hire services for events, construction sites, and more. Our portable solutions are equipped with modern amenities to ensure comfort and convenience, no matter the location or occasion.

Office Furniture

We offer a wide range of stylish and functional office furniture designed to enhance productivity and comfort. From ergonomic chairs and desks to storage solutions and meeting tables, our products combine quality and aesthetics to create the perfect workspace.

Student Furniture

We specialize in durable and ergonomic student furniture designed to create comfortable and productive learning environments. From desks and chairs to storage solutions, our range combines functionality, quality, and affordability for students of all ages.
